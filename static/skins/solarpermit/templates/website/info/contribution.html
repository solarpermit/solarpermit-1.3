Receive recognition for your contributions to SolarPermit.org!

As a registered user, you can receive recognition for your contributions to SolarPermit.org and improve your company's visibility in search engines like Google. If you add your company to your profile on SolarPermit.org, you increase your company's search engine optimization (SEO) in locations where you work.

Here's how:

  1. Log in to your account at SolarPermit.org.

  2. Click Update your Organization Info in the upper right-hand corner of the page.

  3. Search for your organization.

    • Does it show up? If so, Click Add to my profile.
    • Not yet? Click Create New and add your company's name and website.

When you are a top contributor to any SolarPermit.org permitting page, we will recognize your company and link back to your company's website. These links improve your SEO.
